The Software Development Life Cycle is used to make sure that a team of people working on a project for a customer have a solid basis of how to manuver around the workflow and minimize
risk attributed to having multiple people working on a single project. There are many products to help with managing your SDLC such as AWS Developer Tools. Not only will understanding
the SDLC help with programming projects but you can also adapt it to help with business projects or really any team projects in any field
